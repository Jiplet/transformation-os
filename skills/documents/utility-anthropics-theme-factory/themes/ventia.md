# Ventia (2026 Brand)

The official Ventia brand theme as of May 2026. Infrastructure services company operating across utilities, transport, defence, social infrastructure, and resources. Executive-ready, consulting-quality outputs for senior stakeholder audiences.

> **Migration note:** The previous brand was Dark Navy `#0B3254` + Bright Blue `#13B5EA`, font Source Sans Pro. This was retired in May 2026 in favour of the green palette + Calibri shown below. Do not use the legacy palette on new outputs.

## Color Palette

### Core greens

- **Ventia Green** `#009946` — Primary: logo, primary chart series, header bars, hero KPI numbers
- **Forest Green** `#004825` — Headings, body emphasis, dark section panels, contrast strokes
- **Light Green** `#A0CD60` — Accent panels, secondary chart series, subtle fills

### Accent palette (categorical / section dividers)

- **Yellow** `#F5E646` — Accent panel (pair with Forest Green type)
- **Burgundy** `#671C32` — Accent panel (pair with Light Pink)
- **Light Pink** `#EA99AD` — Tonal partner to Burgundy
- **Brick** `#9E3323` — Accent panel (pair with Peach)
- **Peach** `#F7C499` — Tonal partner to Brick

### Neutrals

- **White** `#FFFFFF` — Default background, text on dark fills
- **Black** `#000000` — Default body text
- **Light Grey** `#F2F2F2` — Panel fills, table alt rows, gridlines
- **Mid Grey** `#7A7A7A` — Footnotes, source lines, secondary metadata

## Typography

- **Headers:** Calibri Bold (fallback: Arial Bold)
- **Body Text:** Calibri Regular (fallback: Arial)
- **Web stack:** `Calibri, "Segoe UI", Arial, sans-serif`

### Font Size Hierarchy

| Element | Size | Weight | Colour |
|---|---|---|---|
| Slide title (action title) | 20pt | Bold | Forest Green `#004825` |
| Subtitle / section label | 14pt | Regular | Forest Green `#004825` |
| Body text | 12pt | Regular | Black `#000000` |
| Bullet points | 11pt | Regular | Black `#000000` |
| Chart labels / axis text | 9–10pt | Regular | Black `#000000` |
| Footnotes / source lines | 8pt | Regular | Mid Grey `#7A7A7A` |
| Hero KPI number | 36–44pt | Bold | Ventia Green `#009946` |
| Hero KPI label | 12pt | Regular | Forest Green `#004825` |

## Slide Dimensions

- **Format:** 16:9 widescreen — 13.33" × 7.5"

## Layout Rules

- Left-align all body text and titles (never centre on content slides)
- Title slides may centre the presentation title only
- No all-caps except very short labels (e.g. "FY26", "TOTAL", "INTERNAL")
- Line spacing: 1.15× body text, 1.0× bullet lists
- Maximum 6 bullet points per text block
- Maximum 3 font sizes per slide
- Leave whitespace — resist filling every pixel
- Logo: small Ventia Green logo top-right on content slides; large white REV logo top-left on full-bleed coloured covers
- Header rule: thin Forest Green horizontal line under the logo
- Classification label ("INTERNAL" by default) bottom-left, 8pt Forest Green
- Page number bottom-right, 8pt Mid Grey

## Chart Conventions

### Series Colour Order

1. Ventia Green `#009946`
2. Forest Green `#004825`
3. Light Green `#A0CD60`
4. Yellow `#F5E646`
5. Burgundy `#671C32`
6. Brick `#9E3323`
7. Mid Grey `#7A7A7A`

### Chart Style

- Axis labels: 9pt Calibri, Black
- Gridlines: Light Grey `#F2F2F2`, horizontal only — no vertical gridlines
- No chart border — chart floats on slide background
- Data labels: 9pt, outside end (bars) or above (lines)
- No legend border — text + colour swatch only, max 5 series
- No gradients, shadows, or 3D effects
- No pie charts — use stacked bar or waterfall instead
- Positive variance bars: Ventia Green `#009946`
- Negative variance bars: Burgundy `#671C32`
- Do not pair traffic-light red/amber/green — pair colour with labels instead

## Table Conventions

- Header row: Forest Green `#004825` fill, White text, 11pt Calibri Bold
- Body rows: alternating White / Light Grey `#F2F2F2`, 10pt Calibri regular
- Highlight / totals row: Ventia Green `#009946` fill, White text
- Text columns: left-aligned. Number columns: right-aligned
- No vertical borders. Thin horizontal Light Grey between rows
- Number format: spend `$#,##0` (AUD, no decimals) / counts `#,##0` / percentages `0.0%`

## Slide Conventions

### Action Titles
- Every content slide title is a complete sentence stating the insight
- Good: "Late bookings cost Ventia [$X] in avoidable fare premium"
- Bad: "Booking Analysis" or "Travel Spend Overview"

### Source Line
- Every data slide: left-aligned, y ≈ 6.5"–6.7"
- Format: "Source: [data source], [date/period]"
- 8pt Calibri, Mid Grey `#7A7A7A`

### Callout / Annotation Box
- Light Grey `#F2F2F2` fill, 1pt Forest Green `#004825` border
- 10pt Calibri, regular
- Connector line from callout to relevant data point

### Page Numbers
- Bottom-right corner, 8pt, Mid Grey `#7A7A7A`
- Format: plain number — no "Page X of Y"

## Do Not

- Never use the retired Navy `#0B3254` or Bright Blue `#13B5EA` palette
- Never use Source Sans Pro (retired) — use Calibri
- No gradients, shadows, or 3D effects
- No pie charts
- No clip art or stock photo backgrounds on content slides
- No centre-aligned body text or bullets
- No more than 6 bullets per slide
- No colour alone to convey meaning — pair with labels or icons
- Never use dark backgrounds on content slides (dark green / burgundy reserved for section dividers and covers)
- Currency always AUD (`$#,##0`)

## Best Used For

Executive presentations, strategic analysis decks, procurement and commercial reporting, transformation program updates, data-driven stakeholder briefings — all for senior Ventia leadership audiences.
